Design Discussion

Our website’s design was guided by several key visual principles to create a consistent, professional, and user-friendly experience. First, we used repetition by applying a shared header, navigation bar, and footer across all pages, ensuring consistency and orientation. We also focused on alignment using Bootstrap’s grid system to neatly organize content into responsive sections. This helped us maintain a structured, readable layout across different screen sizes. The use of contrast—such as white text on dark headers, or floated images alongside text—was used to improve visual hierarchy and accessibility. Our color scheme includes a light gradient background to move away from a stark white default and add visual depth without distraction. Typography was addressed by implementing a custom Google Font (Roboto), which gives the text a clean, modern appearance. We also considered balance, placing images to the left and right of text sections to avoid a lopsided layout. Finally, we respected whitespace to prevent content from feeling crowded and enhance readability.

Validation & Testing

  • HTML Validation: All pages were tested and passed using validator.nu.
  • Accessibility: Checked in Chrome Developer Tools for proper heading structure, alt text, and contrast. No major issues were reported.
  • Lighthouse Results: Pages consistently scored above 90 in all four areas (Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO).
